Privacy Policy

Product: AI ColorFix
Effective date:

AI ColorFix is a browser extension that scans web pages for low contrast text and applies on page color adjustments to improve readability and support WCAG contrast goals. This policy explains what data the extension uses and how it is handled.

Summary

  • The extension runs on your device.
  • It does not sell your data.
  • It does not use ads or trackers.
  • Any settings you choose can be saved locally in your browser.

Data we collect

AI ColorFix does not collect personal information such as your name, email, address, or payment details. The extension does not send your browsing history or the content of pages you visit to our servers.

Data the extension processes

To detect and fix contrast issues, the extension reads visual style information on the current page, such as text color and background color. It may also read the text element type and structure needed to apply safe styling changes. This processing happens locally on your device.

Settings and personalization

If you choose a preferred contrast or comfort level, the extension may store these preferences locally using browser extension storage. This data stays in your browser profile and is used only to apply your chosen settings.

Permissions

The extension may request permissions needed to scan and update page styles on the sites you use. This access is used only to detect contrast issues and apply the fixes you request.

Third party services

AI ColorFix does not use third party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy before enabling such services.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data. We only share information if required by law.

Security

We design the extension to minimize data use and keep processing on device. No method of software security is perfect, but we aim to follow good engineering practices to reduce risk.
